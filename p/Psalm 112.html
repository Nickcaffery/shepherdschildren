Psalm 112

1. Praise the Lord! Blessed is the man who fears the Lord, who greatly delights in his commandments!

2. His offspring will be mighty in the land; the generation of the upright will be blessed.

3. Wealth and riches are in his house, and his righteousness endures forever.

4. Light dawns in the darkness for the upright; he is gracious, merciful, and righteous.

5. It is well with the man who deals generously and lends; who conducts his affairs with justice.

6. For the righteous will never be moved; he will be remembered forever.

7. He is not afraid of bad news; his heart is firm, trusting in the Lord.

8. His heart is steady; he will not be afraid, until he looks in triumph on his adversaries.

9. He has distributed freely; he has given to the poor; his righteousness endures forever; his horn is exalted in honor.

10. The wicked man sees it and is angry; he gnashes his teeth and melts away; the desire of the wicked will perish!

Commentary

Psalm 112 is a psalm that praises and describes the blessings of a person who fears and delights in the Lord, who lives a righteous and generous life. It contrasts the fate of the righteous with that of the wicked.

The psalmist begins by praising the Lord and declaring the blessedness of the person who fears the Lord and delights in His commandments. They describe the positive outcomes that result from such a life, such as mighty offspring and generational blessings. The psalmist acknowledges the enduring righteousness of the person who fears the Lord.

The psalmist highlights the character and actions of the righteous person. They describe how light dawns in the darkness for the upright, symbolizing guidance and blessings from God. The righteous person is gracious, merciful, and righteous in their dealings. They are generous, lend to others, and conduct their affairs with justice.

The psalmist affirms that the righteous person will never be moved and will be remembered forever. They express the unwavering trust of the righteous in the Lord, even in the face of bad news. The heart of the righteous person is firm and steady, and they do not fear, anticipating triumph over their adversaries.

The psalmist emphasizes the generosity and compassion of the righteous person. They freely distribute and give to the poor, and their righteousness endures forever. The horn, symbolizing strength and honor, is exalted for the righteous.

The psalm concludes by contrasting the reaction of the wicked person to the blessings of the righteous. The wicked person becomes angry and filled with desire, but their desires will ultimately perish.

Psalm 112 celebrates the blessings and positive outcomes of a life that fears and delights in the Lord. It extols the righteousness, generosity, and unwavering trust of the righteous person, emphasizing their enduring righteousness and the ultimate triumph over their adversaries. It serves as an encouragement for individuals to live a righteous and generous life, knowing that their actions and trust in the Lord will bring blessings and favor.
