Psalm 86

1. Incline your ear, O Lord, and answer me, for I am poor and needy.

2. Preserve my life, for I am godly; save your servant, who trusts in youâ€”you are my God.

3. Be gracious to me, O Lord, for to you do I cry all the day.

4. Gladden the soul of your servant, for to you, O Lord, do I lift up my soul.

5. For you, O Lord, are good and forgiving, abounding in steadfast love to all who call upon you.

6. Give ear, O Lord, to my prayer; listen to my plea for grace.

7. In the day of my trouble I call upon you, for you answer me.

8. There is none like you among the gods, O Lord, nor are there any works like yours.

9. All the nations you have made shall come and worship before you, O Lord, and shall glorify your name.

10. For you are great and do wondrous things; you alone are God.

11. Teach me your way, O Lord, that I may walk in your truth; unite my heart to fear your name.

12. I give thanks to you, O Lord my God, with my whole heart, and I will glorify your name forever.

13. For great is your steadfast love toward me; you have delivered my soul from the depths of Sheol.

14. O God, insolent men have risen up against me; a band of ruthless men seeks my life, and they do not set you before them.

15. But you, O Lord, are a God merciful and gracious, slow to anger and abounding in steadfast love and faithfulness.

16. Turn to me and be gracious to me; give your strength to your servant, and save the son of your maidservant.

17. Show me a sign of your favor, that those who hate me may see and be put to shame because you, Lord, have helped me and comforted me.

Commentary

Psalm 86 is a heartfelt prayer of David, seeking God's mercy, guidance, and protection. The psalmist acknowledges their own poverty and neediness, appealing to God to hear and answer their cry. They express trust in God as their Savior, appealing for His preservation of their life.

The psalmist calls upon God to be gracious and gladden their soul. They lift up their soul to God, recognizing His goodness, forgiveness, and abounding steadfast love for all who call upon Him. The psalmist pleads for God's attentive ear and His grace in response to their prayer. They confidently proclaim that God answers them in the day of trouble.

The psalmist affirms the uniqueness of God among all other gods, acknowledging His unmatched works. They declare that all nations will come to worship and glorify God, recognizing His greatness and the wondrous things He does. They acknowledge God as the one and only true God.

The psalmist seeks God's guidance and instruction, asking to be taught His ways and to walk in His truth. They desire a united heart that fears God's name. The psalmist expresses gratitude to God with their whole heart and commits to glorifying His name forever. They celebrate God's steadfast love, acknowledging that He has delivered their soul from the depths of Sheol, the realm of the dead.

The psalmist acknowledges the opposition they face from insolent and ruthless men who seek their life. They contrast the wickedness of their enemies with the merciful, gracious, slow-to-anger, and steadfast love and faithfulness of God. The psalmist appeals for God's grace, strength, and salvation. They ask for a sign of God's favor, desiring that their enemies may see it and be put to shame. The psalmist finds comfort in God's help and seeks His continued support.

Psalm 86 is a heartfelt plea for God's mercy, guidance, and protection. The psalmist acknowledges their own neediness and proclaims trust in God as their Savior. They celebrate God's goodness, forgiveness, and steadfast love, and seek His instruction and united heart. The psalmist expresses gratitude for God's deliverance and appeals for His continued strength and help against their enemies.
