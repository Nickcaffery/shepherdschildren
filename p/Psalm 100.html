Psalm 100

1. Make a joyful noise to the Lord, all the earth!

2. Serve the Lord with gladness! Come into his presence with singing!

3. Know that the Lord, he is God! It is he who made us, and we are his; we are his people, and the sheep of his pasture.

4. Enter his gates with thanksgiving, and his courts with praise! Give thanks to him; bless his name!

5. For the Lord is good; his steadfast love endures forever, and his faithfulness to all generations.

Commentary

Psalm 100 is a psalm of thanksgiving and joyful praise, calling all the earth to worship the Lord with gladness and gratitude.

The psalmist begins by urging all the earth to make a joyful noise to the Lord. They exhort people to serve the Lord with gladness and come into His presence with singing.

The psalmist emphasizes the knowledge that the Lord is God, the Creator of all things. They affirm that it is the Lord who made us and we belong to Him as His people, likening us to the sheep of His pasture. This recognition of God's ownership and care for His people sets the foundation for joyful worship.

The psalmist instructs the people to enter the Lord's gates with thanksgiving and His courts with praise. They call for gratitude, blessings, and acknowledgment of His name. The psalmist encourages a posture of gratitude and praise in approaching the presence of the Lord.

The psalm concludes with a declaration of the goodness of the Lord. The psalmist affirms that His steadfast love endures forever, and His faithfulness extends to all generations. This statement emphasizes the enduring and faithful nature of God's love and His commitment to His people throughout time.

Psalm 100 serves as a call to joyful worship and gratitude. It invites all the earth to make a joyful noise, serve the Lord with gladness, and come into His presence with singing. The psalm highlights the knowledge of God's sovereignty as the Creator and His ownership of His people. It encourages thanksgiving, praise, and blessings as an expression of gratitude for God's goodness, steadfast love, and faithfulness that spans across all generations.
