Psalm 85

1. Lord, you were favorable to your land; you restored the fortunes of Jacob.

2. You forgave the iniquity of your people; you covered all their sin. Selah

3. You withdrew all your wrath; you turned from your hot anger.

4. Restore us again, O God of our salvation, and put away your indignation toward us!

5. Will you be angry with us forever? Will you prolong your anger to all generations?

6. Will you not revive us again, that your people may rejoice in you?

7. Show us your steadfast love, O Lord, and grant us your salvation.

8. Let me hear what God the Lord will speak, for he will speak peace to his people, to his saints; but let them not turn back to folly.

9. Surely his salvation is near to those who fear him, that glory may dwell in our land.

10. Steadfast love and faithfulness meet; righteousness and peace kiss each other.

11. Faithfulness springs up from the ground, and righteousness looks down from the sky.

12. Yes, the Lord will give what is good, and our land will yield its increase.

13. Righteousness will go before him and make his footsteps a way.

Commentary

Psalm 85 is a prayer for restoration, forgiveness, and the manifestation of God's steadfast love and salvation. The psalmist acknowledges God's past favor and forgiveness toward the people of Israel. They long for God to restore them once again and turn away His anger.

The psalmist implores God to revive the people, so they may rejoice in Him. They call upon God to show His steadfast love and grant them salvation. The psalmist expresses a desire to hear God's words and receive His message of peace, urging the people not to turn back to foolishness or sin. They affirm that God's salvation is near to those who fear Him, leading to the glory of His presence dwelling in the land.

The psalmist depicts the meeting of steadfast love and faithfulness, as well as the unity of righteousness and peace. They describe how faithfulness springs up from the ground, while righteousness looks down from the sky. The psalmist declares that the Lord will give what is good, and the land will yield its increase. Righteousness will go before God and pave the way for His footsteps.

Psalm 85 expresses a plea for restoration and forgiveness. The psalmist recognizes God's past favor and forgiveness, appealing for a renewed manifestation of His steadfast love and salvation. They desire to hear God's words of peace and pray for the people to walk in righteousness and faithfulness. The psalmist expresses confidence that God will bring goodness and fruitfulness to the land, with righteousness leading the way before Him.
