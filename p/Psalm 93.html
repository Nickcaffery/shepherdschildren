Psalm 93

1. The Lord reigns; he is robed in majesty; the Lord is robed; he has put on strength as his belt. Yes, the world is established; it shall never be moved.

2. Your throne is established from of old; you are from everlasting.

3. The floods have lifted up, O Lord, the floods have lifted up their voice; the floods lift up their roaring.

4. Mightier than the thunders of many waters, mightier than the waves of the sea, the Lord on high is mighty!

5. Your decrees are very trustworthy; holiness befits your house, O Lord, forevermore.

Commentary

Psalm 93 is a short psalm that proclaims the reign and majesty of the Lord. It emphasizes the stability and power of God's throne and highlights the trustworthiness of His decrees.

The psalmist begins by declaring that the Lord reigns. They depict God as being robed in majesty, symbolizing His authority and sovereignty. The psalmist acknowledges that the Lord is strong and that He has established the world in an unshakable manner.

The psalmist affirms that God's throne has been established from ancient times, indicating His eternal reign. They acknowledge that God is from everlasting, emphasizing His timeless existence and unchanging nature.

The psalmist uses the metaphor of floods to describe the powerful and tumultuous forces of nature. They recognize that the floods have lifted up their voice and are roaring. However, the psalmist asserts that the Lord is mightier than the thunders of many waters and the waves of the sea. They emphasize that the Lord on high is mighty and holds ultimate authority and power over all creation.

The psalmist acknowledges the trustworthiness of God's decrees. They recognize that His word is reliable and can be fully trusted. The psalmist affirms that holiness befits God's house, indicating the inherent purity and righteousness of His dwelling place.

The psalm concludes by stating that the Lord's reign and holiness are everlasting. It affirms that the Lord's rule and righteousness will endure forever.

Psalm 93 proclaims the eternal reign and majestic power of the Lord. It emphasizes His unshakable authority and the trustworthiness of His decrees. The psalm reminds readers of the stability and might of God in the face of tumultuous forces and declares that holiness characterizes His dwelling place. It serves as a declaration of praise and an acknowledgment of God's eternal nature and divine sovereignty.
