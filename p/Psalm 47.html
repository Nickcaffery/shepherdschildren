Psalm 47

1. Clap your hands, all peoples! Shout to God with loud songs of joy!

2. For the Lord, the Most High, is to be feared, a great king over all the earth.

3. He subdued peoples under us, and nations under our feet.

4. He chose our heritage for us, the pride of Jacob whom he loves. Selah

5. God has gone up with a shout, the Lord with the sound of a trumpet.

6. Sing praises to God, sing praises! Sing praises to our King, sing praises!

7. For God is the King of all the earth; sing praises with a psalm!

8. God reigns over the nations; God sits on his holy throne.

9. The princes of the peoples gather as the people of the God of Abraham. For the shields of the earth belong to God; he is highly exalted!

Commentary

Psalm 47 is a hymn of praise and exaltation, inviting all peoples to clap their hands and shout to God with joyful songs. The psalmist declares that the Lord, the Most High, is to be feared, for He is a great king over all the earth. They acknowledge God's power in subduing peoples and nations, and they recognize that God has chosen their heritage, the pride of Jacob, whom He loves.

The psalmist describes God's ascent with a shout and the sound of a trumpet. They call for singing praises and exhort others to join in singing praises to God, the King of all the earth. The psalmist affirms that God reigns over the nations and sits on His holy throne. They depict the princes of the peoples gathering together as the people of the God of Abraham. The psalmist emphasizes that the shields of the earth belong to God, signifying His sovereign authority and supreme power. They declare that God is highly exalted.

Psalm 47 serves as a call to worship and praise, celebrating God as the great king over all the earth. The psalmist calls for enthusiastic and joyful expressions of worship, acknowledging God's power and authority. They proclaim God's reign over the nations and emphasize His exalted position. The psalm invites all people to join in the exaltation of God and acknowledges that all earthly powers ultimately belong to Him.
