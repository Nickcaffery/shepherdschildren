Psalm 136

1. Give thanks to the Lord, for he is good, for his steadfast love endures forever.

2. Give thanks to the God of gods, for his steadfast love endures forever.

3. Give thanks to the Lord of lords, for his steadfast love endures forever;

4. to him who alone does great wonders, for his steadfast love endures forever;

5. to him who by understanding made the heavens, for his steadfast love endures forever;

6. to him who spread out the earth above the waters, for his steadfast love endures forever;

7. to him who made the great lights, for his steadfast love endures forever;

8. the sun to rule over the day, for his steadfast love endures forever;

9. the moon and stars to rule over the night, for his steadfast love endures forever;

10. to him who struck down the firstborn of Egypt, for his steadfast love endures forever;

11. and brought Israel out from among them, for his steadfast love endures forever;

12. with a strong hand and an outstretched arm, for his steadfast love endures forever;

13. to him who divided the Red Sea in two, for his steadfast love endures forever;

14. and made Israel pass through the midst of it, for his steadfast love endures forever;

15. but overthrew Pharaoh and his host in the Red Sea, for his steadfast love endures forever;

16. to him who led his people through the wilderness, for his steadfast love endures forever;

17. to him who struck down great kings, for his steadfast love endures forever;

18. and killed mighty kings, for his steadfast love endures forever;

19. Sihon, king of the Amorites, for his steadfast love endures forever;

20. and Og, king of Bashan, for his steadfast love endures forever;

21. and gave their land as a heritage, for his steadfast love endures forever;

22. a heritage to Israel his servant, for his steadfast love endures forever.

23. It is he who remembered us in our low estate, for his steadfast love endures forever;

24. and rescued us from our foes, for his steadfast love endures forever;

25. he who gives food to all flesh, for his steadfast love endures forever.

26. Give thanks to the God of heaven, for his steadfast love endures forever.

Commentary

Psalm 136 is a psalm of thanksgiving and praise that repeatedly affirms the enduring and steadfast love of the Lord. Each verse begins with the proclamation, "for his steadfast love endures forever," emphasizing the everlasting nature of God's love.

The psalmist gives thanks to the Lord, the God of gods and Lord of lords, recognizing His supremacy and faithfulness. The psalmist acknowledges the Lord's greatness and wisdom displayed in the creation of the heavens, the earth, and the celestial bodies. They praise the Lord for His wondrous works and His ordering of the universe.

The psalmist recalls the Lord's mighty acts of deliverance, specifically the liberation of Israel from Egypt. They remember how the Lord struck down the firstborn of Egypt and brought Israel out with a strong hand and an outstretched arm. They recount the miraculous parting of the Red Sea, the destruction of Pharaoh and his army, and the guidance of Israel through the wilderness.

The psalmist acknowledges the Lord's defeat of great kings and mighty nations, such as Sihon, king of the Amorites, and Og, king of Bashan. They recognize that the Lord gave Israel their land as a heritage, a possession and inheritance for His people.

The psalmist praises the Lord for His remembrance of Israel in their low estate and His deliverance from their enemies. They acknowledge the Lord's provision of food for all living creatures.

The psalm concludes with a final exhortation to give thanks to the God of heaven, affirming once again that His steadfast love endures forever.

Psalm 136 is a psalm that celebrates the enduring and faithful love of the Lord. It offers gratitude for His wondrous works in creation and His acts of deliverance on behalf of His people. The psalmist emphasizes the everlasting nature of God's love, calling for continual thanksgiving and praise to the God whose steadfast love endures forever.
