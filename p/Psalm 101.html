Psalm 101

1. I will sing of steadfast love and justice; to you, O Lord, I will make music.

2. I will ponder the way that is blameless. Oh when will you come to me? I will walk with integrity of heart within my house;

3. I will not set before my eyes anything that is worthless. I hate the work of those who fall away; it shall not cling to me.

4. A perverse heart shall be far from me; I will know nothing of evil.

5. Whoever slanders his neighbor secretly I will destroy. Whoever has a haughty look and an arrogant heart I will not endure.

6. I will look with favor on the faithful in the land, that they may dwell with me; he who walks in the way that is blameless shall minister to me.

7. No one who practices deceit shall dwell in my house; no one who utters lies shall continue before my eyes.

8. Morning by morning I will destroy all the wicked in the land, cutting off all the evildoers from the city of the Lord.

Commentary

Psalm 101 is a psalm of commitment to righteousness and justice. The psalmist declares their intention to sing of steadfast love and justice and to live with integrity and purity of heart.

The psalmist begins by expressing their desire to sing of God's steadfast love and justice and make music to Him. They express their longing for God to come to them, pondering a blameless way of living.

The psalmist resolves to walk with integrity of heart within their own house. They declare that they will not set their eyes on anything that is worthless and they hate the work of those who fall away from righteousness. The psalmist determines not to be influenced or associated with those who have perverse hearts or engage in evil.

The psalmist condemns slander, vowing to destroy those who secretly slander their neighbors. They also refuse to tolerate those with haughty looks and arrogant hearts.

The psalmist expresses favor and solidarity towards the faithful in the land, desiring their companionship and ministry. They acknowledge the value of walking in a blameless way and commit to associating with those who do so.

The psalmist declares that no one who practices deceit or utters lies will dwell in their house or continue before their eyes. They establish a standard of truthfulness and integrity.

The psalmist affirms their commitment to deal with the wicked and evildoers in the land. They express their determination to confront and destroy the wicked, cutting off evildoers from the city of the Lord.

Psalm 101 serves as a declaration of commitment to righteousness, integrity, and justice. The psalmist expresses their desire to live blamelessly and avoid association with evil. They condemn slander, arrogance, deceit, and lies, affirming their commitment to truthfulness. The psalmist also asserts their determination to confront and remove the wicked from their midst. The psalm presents a vision of a just and righteous lifestyle, calling for a commitment to upright living.
